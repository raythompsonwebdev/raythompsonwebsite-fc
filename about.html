What I do

I code in my spare time in the evenings and at weekends and during the day when I am temporarily out of work. I practice building websites and web applications using languages HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP and MySQL.

I have spent lots of spare time teaching myself web development and programming. I have watched lots of video tutorials, listened to podcasts and read blogs and books relating to coding.

I attended a WordPress WordCamp in London in 2018 as a volunteer and made a samll contribution to the Theme Review Team helpingthem review WordPress Themes. I have also attended various on-line WordPress conferences like WordCampEU 2020 Contributors Day held recently to give members of the public a chance find out more about the review process WordPress themes submitted by other developers go through before being added to Wordpress.org theme directory.
